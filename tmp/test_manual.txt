Product Manual - Test Device

Welcome to the Test Device user manual.

The Test Device is a simple electronic device with the following features:
- Power button: Press to turn on/off
- LED indicator: Shows device status (green=on, red=off)
- Battery life: Up to 8 hours of continuous use
- Charging: Use the included USB cable

Troubleshooting:
- If device won't turn on, check battery level
- If LED is blinking red, device needs charging
- For reset, hold power button for 10 seconds

Safety Information:
- Do not expose to water
- Keep away from extreme temperatures
- Use only approved charging cables
